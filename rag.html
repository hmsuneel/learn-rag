2. Chunk the documents

Break long documents into retrieval-sized pieces. Chunk size and overlap affect answer quality.

3. Create embeddings

This simulator creates deterministic pseudo-embeddings for visualization. Production systems use embedding models such as OpenAI, Cohere, Voyage, BGE, or E5.
